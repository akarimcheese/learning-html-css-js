NOVSBA

Northern Virginia Scholastic Bowling Association

News

Need funding for your high school club? The Northern Virginia Scholastic Bowling Association is currently planning an LCPS club bowling invitational! As of now, the concept is that any LCPS high school club can sign up to participate in a bowling competition that will be 2 rounds: a preliminary round and a final round consisting of the top 2 clubs. Both rounds will be played in a 5-team format, though the first round will be scored by overall points whereas the final round will be played in a traditional NOVSBA head-to-head format. The winning club will receive $500 dollars for club funding. Teams will need to pay for their own games, but teams in the final round will have their game paid for.
