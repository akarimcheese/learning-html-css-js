NOVSBA

Northern Virginia Scholastic Bowling Association

About

The Northern Virginia Scholastic Bowling Association is an independent organization run by volunteers of the Northern Virginia scholastic community. NOVSBA hopes to provide to provide year-round team bowling programs using commercial bowling facilities. NOVSBA was founded by students in 2014 and remains to be run by student, teachers, and alumni.

Mission

The NOVSBA mission is to provide young adults of Northern Virginia with an opportunity to learn sportsmanship, teamwork, and bowling in a safe and friendly environment. NOVSBA hopes to develop respect for others and self-confidence. NOVSBA emphasizes the importance of being a role model for opposing teams by demonstrating exceptional sportsmanship. NOVSBA provides an environment to develop the fundamentals of bowling as well as to develop skills to succeed in a team environment.
